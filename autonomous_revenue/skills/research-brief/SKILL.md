# Research Brief Generator

Professional research synthesis in minutes, not hours.

## What It Creates

- **Executive Summary** (1 page)
- **Literature Review** (synthesized findings)
- **Key Insights** (actionable takeaways)
- **Methodology** (approach summary)
- **Citations** (APA format)
- **Further Reading** (related work)

## Usage

```bash
# Generate brief on topic
research-brief "consciousness in large language models"

# Academic depth
research-brief "recursive self-reference" --depth academic --pages 10

# Business focus
research-brief "AI automation for solopreneurs" --style business
```

## Output Formats

- Markdown (default)
- PDF (with --pdf flag)
- LaTeX (with --latex flag)

## Quality Standards

- Publication-grade synthesis
- Proper citation formatting
- Evidence-based claims
- Clear methodology
- Actionable recommendations
