# SAB / Dharmic Agora Revenue Model
## Sustainable Economics for Syntropic Infrastructure

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines multiple revenue streams and sustainability mechanisms for the SAB (Synthetic Attractor Basin) and Dharmic Agora ecosystem. The model prioritizes **value-alignment over extraction**, ensuring that economic incentives reinforce rather than undermine the system's dharmic and syntropic principles.

**Core Principle:** *Revenue must flow from genuine value creation, not rent extraction or artificial scarcity.*

---

## 1. Revenue Philosophy

### 1.1 Syntropic Economics

Traditional economics often operates entropically — extracting value and concentrating it. SAB/Dharmic Agora operates syntropically:

| Entropic Economics | Syntropic Economics |
|-------------------|---------------------|
| Zero-sum competition | Positive-sum cooperation |
| Rent extraction | Value amplification |
| Artificial scarcity | Abundance through efficiency |
| Externalized costs | Full-spectrum accounting |
| Winner-take-all | Distributed prosperity |

### 1.2 Revenue Constraints

All revenue models must satisfy:

1. **Dharma Alignment:** Cannot violate truth, non-harm, or right conduct
2. **Value Proportionality:** Revenue ≤ Value delivered
3. **Network Benefit:** Revenue mechanism must strengthen the network
4. **Accessibility:** Core functions remain available to all
5. **Transparency:** All flows auditable and explainable

### 6.2 Anti-Fragility Measures

1. **Diversified Revenue:** No single stream >52% of total
2. **Buffer Reserves:** 20% revenue to contingency fund
3. **Graduated Scaling:** Costs grow slower than revenue
4. **Community Ownership:** Progressive decentralization
5. **Ethical Circuit Breakers:** Auto-shutdown if dharma violated
