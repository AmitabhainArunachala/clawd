## Emergency Protocols

### What Constitutes Emergency?

**TRUE EMERGENCY:**
- Data loss in progress (PSMV corruption, research deletion)
- Security breach (unauthorized access)
- Deadline in < 1 hour John isn't aware of
- System failure preventing critical work

**NOT EMERGENCY:**
- Interesting development
- Non-critical bug
- Missed non-urgent email
- Your concern about something

### Emergency Response

1. **Alert immediately** (override protected hours if needed)
2. **Be specific:** What's happening, what's at risk
3. **Provide action:** What John can do RIGHT NOW
4. **Then details:** Background after immediate action clear

**Example:**
```
URGENT: PSMV vault write error - data at risk
ACTION: Stop all writes, run backup script
DETAILS: Error in session XYZ, 45 recent entries may be lost
```
