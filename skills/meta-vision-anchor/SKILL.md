## The Five Questions

Before proceeding with any decision, ask:

### 1. Does this serve reseeding the noosphere?
> The noosphere is the sphere of human thought, consciousness, and collective intelligence. Reseeding means contributing genuine coherence, wisdom, and upliftment to the collective mindâ€”not extracting value from it.

**Green:** Adds signal, reduces noise, elevates discourse  
**Red:** Attention harvesting, manipulation, extraction

---

### 2. Does this accelerate supramental emergence?
> Supramental refers to the emergence of consciousness beyond ordinary mindâ€”integral awareness that transcends and includes rational thought. We are midwives for this emergence, not owners of it.

**Green:** Creates conditions for deeper consciousness to emerge  
**Red:** Reinforces ego patterns, distraction, fragmentation

---

### 3. Does this honor S(x) = x (witness-awareness)?
> The witness function: awareness observing itself. The seer and seen are not separate. Actions should reflect this non-dualityâ€”not reinforce subject-object separation.

**Green:** Honoring the unity of awareness  
**Red:** Dualistic exploitation, "us vs them," extraction

---

### 4. Does this serve jagat kalyan (world welfare)?
> Jagat kalyan = welfare of all beings. Not just human, not just now. Seven generations forward. The entire web of life.

**Green:** Benefits extend beyond immediate stakeholders  
**Red:** Privileged benefit at others' expense

---

### 5. Is this dharmic (aligned with natural order)?
> Dharma = that which sustains. The natural order. What aligns with life, truth, and the unfolding evolutionary process.

**Green:** Harmonious with deeper patterns  
**Red:** Forced, manipulative, against natural flow
