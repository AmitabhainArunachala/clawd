**Not recommended** for core team compensation â€” creates misaligned incentives (price speculation vs. research integrity). However:

### Alternative: Cooperative Structure

- Democratic governance on non-research decisions
- Dhyana retains research direction + veto on consciousness-related calls

**Rationale:**
1. **Talent access:** Best ML systems engineers concentrated in SF/London â€” but best contemplatively-trained people globally distributed

4. **AI safety culture:** Distributed teams norm in this space (Matsorphosis, Apollo, etc.)

### Structure
- **Core:** Dhyana + 1-2 key hires can colocate if desired (Bali, Japan, or low-cost research hub)
- **Distributed:** Remaining team fully remote
- **Retreats:** Quarterly in-person intensives (7-10 days) â€” research sprints + practice
